ABOUT US

Mikes Donuts is a family owned and run business dating back to 1976. Mike and his wife Christina immigrated from Greece with the American Dream in mind and achieved it. Today, with 3 generations of workers including Mike's son,daughter, and grandsons.

LOCATION

Mikes Donuts is loacted in Bay Ride, Brooklyn at 6822 5th Avenue. We are open 7 days a week from 4:00AM-6:00PM.From 1976 the only time Mike's is ever closed is New Years and Christmas. Due to the CoronaVirus pandemic we have temporarily closed down but plan on repopening the first week of May!

PRESS

Throughout the years Mike's has gained multiple rewards such as being ranked on the best donuts in New York as well as competing in multiple donut competitions. We have also had the honor to host many celeberties and TV series most notable Impractical Jokers and Shades of Blue.
